# Frequently Asked Questions

## What is timeboxing?

Timeboxing is a time management method where you divide your day into fixed blocks of time, each dedicated to a specific task. Instead of working from a to-do list and hoping you finish everything, you plan *when* and *how long* you'll work on each task. Dayopt makes this process simple and adds reflection to help you improve over time.

## Is Dayopt free?

Yes. The Free plan gives you up to 3 projects, 1GB of storage, basic analytics, and API access — no credit card required. You can use Dayopt for free as long as you want.

## What does the Pro plan include?

For $5/month, Pro gives you unlimited projects, 100GB storage, advanced analytics with custom reports, team collaboration features, custom integrations, and priority support.

## Does Dayopt sync with Google Calendar?

Yes. Dayopt integrates with Google Calendar so your timeboxes appear alongside your existing events. Changes sync both ways.

## How does the Accuracy Score work?

The Accuracy Score compares your planned timeboxes with what you actually did. If you planned 2 hours of deep work and completed 1.5 hours, your score reflects that gap. Over time, you can see patterns in how accurately you estimate and where your plans tend to break down.

## What is Energy Mapping?

After completing a timebox, you can optionally log your focus and energy level. Over time, Dayopt shows you when you tend to be most productive — your peak hours, dip periods, and recovery windows. This helps you schedule your most important work at the right time.

## Is my data private?

Yes. Dayopt does not sell your data, does not show ads, and does not use your data for training AI models. Your data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest. You can delete your account and all data at any time — it's permanently removed within 30 days.

## Can I delete my account?

Yes. Go to Settings > Account and click "Delete Account." All your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

## Can I export my data?

Yes. You can export your plans and records as CSV or JSON from the Settings page.

## Is there a mobile app?

Dayopt is a web application optimized for both desktop and mobile browsers. A dedicated mobile app is on the roadmap.

## Who builds Dayopt?

Dayopt is built by an indie developer. No VC funding, no data selling, no ad-driven business model. Just a product you pay for (or use free) that respects your time and your data.
